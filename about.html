About

This site

This website is a small companion to our Fitness and Wellness Center. We made it so students, staff, and guests can feel welcome the moment they walk in, and leave with a clearer sense of how to train well on their own time.

Magis is not only doing more, but doing what matters with care. Here that means honoring the body as part of the whole person: mind, spirit, and community. The QR guides beside our equipment are meant to help you move safely, learn at your pace, and grow steady habits that carry beyond the gym floor.

Whether you are trying equipment for the first time or returning after a busy term, we hope this space helps you stay curious, train wisely, and keep wellness woven into everyday life on campus.
