Lat Pulldown Machine

Muscle hit — Lats (upper back) + Biceps

Lat pulldown (beginner)

  1. Sit tall. Adjust the thigh pad so your legs are secure and your hands reach overhead.
  2. Grab the bar (wide or shoulder-width). Keep shoulders down and chest up.
  3. Pull the bar down to your upper chest. Pause, then return slowly until your arms are straight again.
